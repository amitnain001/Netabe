Why and Why Now?

Companies are struggling with subscribing, deploying, maintaining a slew of software leading to rigidity, fragmentation, divergent user experiences, and spiraling costs. And despite hundreds of applications, the reliance on email - an antiquated killer app that is more hindrance than help – and usage of spreadsheets is ever present.

Netabe.com is a web application development platform that will allow users to compose and share “web apps” as easily as composing and sharing emails. But with the specific and necessary functionality of an app with the pain of development, deployment, maintenance, and upgrade.

Netabe enables a friction-free ecosystem of purpose-built applications with personalized functionality without reliance on IT Staff.

  • No domain names to purchase/renew
  • No DNS to configure
  • No Web Servers/Hosting to provision
  • No programming languages to learn
  • No coding/testing/debugging/deployment

And why IT Teams and CISO (Chief Information Security Officers) love Netabe?

  • No additional software development or integrations.
  • No sites to breach
  • No Email IDs to spoof
  • Sharing is limited to Authenticated Trust Circles
